Pressure to Wavenumber Conversion

Detailed Operation

Laser Setup

Select the wavelength of the laser used in your experiment:

  • 488nm: Argon-ion laser
  • 514.5nm: Gold standard for ruby
  • 532nm: Solid-state laser
  • 633nm: HeNe laser
  • 785nm: For special measurements

Pressure Input

Enter the pressure value in Gigapascals (GPa):

  • Valid range: 0 to 500 GPa
  • Use dot or comma as decimal separator
  • Calculation is performed automatically

Advanced Features

Calibration Adjustment

Compensate for instrumental variations with fine adjustment:

  1. Select the desired laser
  2. Use + and - buttons to adjust
  3. The value is automatically saved per laser
